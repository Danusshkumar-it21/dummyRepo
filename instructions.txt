1. In a medium-sized pot, bring 1 1/2 cups of water to a boil.
2. Add the Maggi noodles to the boiling water and let them cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
3. Turn off the heat and add the Maggi tastemaker masala to the pot.
4. Mix well until the masala is evenly distributed and the noodles are coated in the seasoning.
5. Serve hot and enjoy!
